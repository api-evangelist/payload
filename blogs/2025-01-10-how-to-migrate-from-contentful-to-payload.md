A walkthrough for migrating from Contentful to Payload 3.0, covering data export via CLI, project setup, and handling complex content like rich text and media files. The guide highlights that, unlike SaaS Contentful, Payload is open source and can be self-hosted so your data stays with you; it was created by Payload partner 10xmedia.
